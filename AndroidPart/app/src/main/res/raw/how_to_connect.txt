1. Bluetooth should be enabled on both the phone and laptop/computer
2. Connect the phone to laptop/computer via Bluetooth
3. If device name of the laptop/computer is not shown on the main page of the phone app, go to "Configure Bluetooth" and select that name in the "Paired" list
4. In the desktop app, click "Discover paired" or "Discover all" and select device name of the phone in the "Paired" list - it will appear near the "Connect" button
5. Click "Connect" in the desktop app
6. In the phone app, go to "Touchscreen" and click the Play button
7. When the connection establishes, the touchscreen gets the aspect ratio of the laptop/computer screen and the directions are written on it; in the desktop app the darker frame changes color and begins approximately showing the location of your gestures
